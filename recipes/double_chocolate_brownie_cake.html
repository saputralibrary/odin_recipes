Double Chocolate Brownie Cake

Original Page Source

Photo by

This chocolate brownie cake is easy to make with cake mix, instant pudding, sour cream, and chocolate chips. Chocolate lovers beware: it is very yummy!

  • Ingredients
  • Directions

  • Ingredients

    1. 1 (15.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix
    2. 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
    3. 4 large eggs
    4. 1 cup sour cream
    5. ½ cup vegetable oil
    6. ½ cup water
    7. 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

  • Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan. Have all ingredients at room temperature.

    2. Stir cake mix and pudding mix together in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and pour in eggs, sour cream, oil, and water. Beat on low speed until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat 4 minutes on medium speed. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared pan.

    3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 50 to 60 minutes. Allow to cool.
